About Irrigation

Irrigation is the artificial application of water to the soil to help grow crops, maintain landscapes, and revegetate disturbed soils.

Types of Irrigation

Surface Irrigation

Water is distributed over and across land by gravity, without mechanical pumps.

Drip Irrigation

Water drips slowly to the roots of plants through a network of valves, pipes, and emitters.

Sprinkler Irrigation

Water is distributed through a system of pipes and sprayed into the air to fall like rain.
