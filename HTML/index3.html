Irrigation Methods

Explore different types of irrigation techniques

Drip Irrigation

Delivers water directly to the roots of plants through a system of tubes, valves, and emitters. Highly efficient for water conservation.

Sprinkle Irrigation

Sprays water over the field like rainfall using a system of pipes and sprinklers. Suitable for many types of crops.

Surface Irrigation

Water flows over the soil surface to the crops. Common in traditional farming and cost-effective but less efficient.

Subsurface Irrigation

Water is delivered below the soil surface through a network of buried pipes. Efficient but costly and complex to install.
